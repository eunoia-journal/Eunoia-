Privacy Policy

All journal entries and personal information are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Your data is not sent to any server and is not accessible to anyone else.

No Data Sharing

We do not collect, track, sell, or share your personal data with any third party.

Your Control

You can clear or delete your data at any time by clearing your browser data.
